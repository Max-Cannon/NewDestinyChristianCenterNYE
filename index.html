NYE Event Details

Worship, Music & Message

Join Pastor Paula White-Cain and Jonathan Cain for our New Year's Eve celebration at New Destiny Christian Center, starting at 9:30 pm. Come experience a night of music, worship, laughter, and Christ-centered inspiration for the new year. Your spirit will be encouraged by an anointed worship service with Greg Brunot, after which you will be treated to the renditions of master rapper "OZ". Next Jonathan Cain of the epic rock group Journey will play his favorite Motown songs in concert. Finally, you will top the evening off with a powerful prophetic word from Pastor Paula!

Post Midnight After Party

At 12:01 pm, the "after-party" festivities get under way which will include FREE food,  fun games, fellowship and dancing as your soul is further energized with a live DJ spinning your favorite dance tunes.

Countdown to 2018

As the clock ticks down to the start of 2018, take a moment to reflect upon what good God has brought your way in 2017. Sometimes what we see as a setback has now been revealed to be a blessing in retrospect. Look forward then with great anticipation in achieving all that God has destined you to become in the coming year!
